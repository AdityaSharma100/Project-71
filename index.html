Auditorium

Once, our Annual function was held in the auditorium. Then we all sang prayers. A short play was organised by the students of class VII for us. We listened a short documentery on the life of Jesus on a projecter. That Annual function was one of the best memory of all.



Library

Once, when we were in III grade our teacher took us to the library to read some books. She had distrubuted the children into groups, each group sat on a table and shared what he/she had read. That was a really good experience.
